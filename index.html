about

I dream of a world where the first question on designers' minds is "who can we better include?"

By innovating inclusively, we can radically expand the human experience. In working towards this vision, I care about working on mission-driven design questions, especially in terms of responding to marginalized perspectives. In the past, I've focused on empowering disabled women, transgender folks, and indigenous communities through physical and digital design.

Right now, I'm reading Design Justice by Sasha Costanza-Chock and exploring dairy alternatives. Send recommendations my way!
